About me

Back-end developer specializing in C#/.NET. Learned my coding skills at The Iron Yard coding academy, from which I graduated in September, 2017. Previously worked as a broadcast consulting engineer for twelve years, during which I helped radio and TV stations optimize their coverage, minimize interference with other broadcast facilities, select optimal channels/frequencies through allocation studies, and ensure that the station's environmental impact complied with FCC regulations, including human exposure to radiofrequency radiation.


I am also a musician. My musical background includes being the Cantor of Congregation B'nai Israel in St. Petersburg, Florida, performance experience in operas, operettas, musicals and with contemporary a cappella groups. Instruments include guitar, violin and hand drums.
